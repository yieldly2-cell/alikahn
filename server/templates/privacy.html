2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Account creation and management — To register your account, verify your identity via email OTP, and maintain your account profile
  • Deposit and withdrawal processing — To verify and approve financial transactions through manual administrator review
  • Investment tracking — To manage 72-hour investment cycles, calculate profits based on your yield rate, and credit matured investment payouts
  • Referral program administration — To track referral activity, apply yield rate increases, and distribute referral bonuses
  • Security and fraud prevention — To enforce account restrictions, detect suspicious activity, and maintain platform integrity through device ID verification
  • Administrative auditing — To maintain audit logs of administrative actions for accountability and dispute resolution

Your data may only be disclosed in the following limited circumstances:

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Password protection: All passwords are securely hashed using the bcrypt algorithm before storage. We never store passwords in plain text, and our administrators cannot view your password.
  • Authentication: Session management is handled through JSON Web Tokens (JWT) with secure signing, ensuring that only authenticated users can access their account data.
  • Login protection: Rate limiting is applied to login attempts to prevent brute-force attacks. Accounts are temporarily locked after multiple failed attempts.
  • Email verification: One-time passwords (OTPs) with a 10-minute expiry and limited retry attempts protect the registration process.
  • File security: Uploaded deposit screenshots are stored securely with randomized filenames to prevent unauthorized access.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you acknowledge that you provide your information at your own risk.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data in accordance with the following policies:

Active Account Data

Your personal information (name, email, device ID, referral data) is retained for as long as your account remains active on the Platform. You may request account deletion at any time by contacting us (see Section 8).

Financial Records

Deposit records, withdrawal records, investment history, and associated screenshots are retained for a minimum of 2 years from the date of the transaction. This retention period is maintained for audit, compliance, and dispute resolution purposes.

Audit Logs

Administrative action logs are retained for security and accountability purposes and may be kept for up to 2 years.

Account Deletion

Upon receiving a valid account deletion request, we will delete your personal account data within 30 days. Financial transaction records may be retained for the 2-year retention period as described above, even after account deletion, in anonymized or minimal form as necessary for compliance.
